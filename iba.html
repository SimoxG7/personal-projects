About the IBA
The International Bartenders Association was founded on Saturday, 24th February 1951 in the Saloon of the Grand Hotel in Torquay, United Kingdom. Still today this event is celebrated all over the world as the International Bartenders’ Day.
The Association’s main aims are to promote and maintain International Relations among the Guild Members and to encourage a high standard of competence and conduct for the benefit and advancement of the profession of Bartending.
IBA Official Cocktail List
The official IBA Cocktail List recognizes the most requested recipes, used over the years by bartenders servicing the most important bars all over the world. This list has led to a grand evolution in the world of mixed drinks.

WCC2022

The IBA World Cocktail Championship 2022 is set to be the biggest reunion of the World’s greatest Cocktail and Flair Bartenders!
Incorporating all 64 countries of the IBA, The WCC will be a battle of the best. Cocktails will be flowing and the skill & entertainment will be absolutely Incredible!

Our hosting country will be the Beautiful Cuba in the vibrant area of Varadero, led by the incredible Asociación de Cantineros de Cuba.
National level Cocktail and Flair Competitions will be held in each of our 64 IBA countries in the search for the next IBA World Cocktail Champion.

Get your Shakers out and start training in your routines for this Major Global Cocktail Championship. Now is your time to shine on the world stage, See you in Cuba 2022!
