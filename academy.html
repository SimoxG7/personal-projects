IBA Academy
Welcome to the next generation of the IBA Academy. We know how important it is to the best you can be.

To combat the continued growth of the bar industry and the never ending need for a well equipped and talented bartenders.

The IBA has created an Academy to raise the bartenders knowledge and to prepare both the new and established bartenders and prepare them for all aspects of the bar industry.

We have different courses depending on your needs and have created an all encompassing textbook to hand in hand with the courses or for you to learn on your own.

The book is a study guide, a textbook, a reference of ideas, philosophies, and best practices for those looking to learn how to bartend as well as for those who have years of experience.

Our aim is to elevate the overall consistency, professionalism, and knowledge of bartenders world wide.

With such a wide and diverse global society that makes up the IBA, we are excited for this manual to link all our members and the bartending community at large to help unite us and help guide consistency as well as creativity through education.
