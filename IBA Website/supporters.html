As an independent entity, we are an ideal platform for all the brands to present themselves and to provide competing bartenders with as wide a choice of products as possible.

By becoming an Associate member of the IBA, brands increase their worldwide awareness among the bartender community, expand their reach and visibility in the on-trade. In return, bartenders throughout the world have used our Associate Members brands to create amazing cocktails.

This brand engagement has led many bartenders to become the best ambassadors to give global reach to many smaller industry players. We started preparations for the World Cocktail Championship 2022 that will come back with a refreshed format.

We would like to take this opportunity to invite your company to join us as Associate Members.
